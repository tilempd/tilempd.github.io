Obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Physics and a Master of Science in Physical Oceanography within the Ocean Physics and Modelling Group at the University of Athens, advised by Professor Sarantis Sofianos.

Currently undertaking a Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering at Cornell University as a member of the Environmental C.F.D. Group, advised by Professor Peter Diamessis.

Research focuses on the dynamics and transport induced by unstable internal solitary waves of depression shoaling over gentle slopes, using high-accuracy/resolution spectral-element simulations and Lagrangian diagnostics.
